Welcome to KratinCare+

KratinCare+ is a web application designed to improve your health and well-being. We understand that staying healthy and active is essential for a better life.

With KratinCare+, you can:

  • Receive timely medication reminders to ensure you never miss a dose.
  • Access a wide range of exercise and yoga videos for staying active and flexible.
  • Track your medication schedule and dosage for better health management.
  • Connect and chat with other users to share experiences and motivate each other.
  • Manage your profile information and preferences.
  • Get personalized health tips and suggestions based on your activity and preferences.
